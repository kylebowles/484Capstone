About us

CUED-In Inc. is an app and web-based platform that fosters a career-focused, community connected future. We create a common site for students, counselors, teachers, businesses, and parents to collaborate throughout the college and career search making the process straightforward and efficient. We help students succeed after high school by linking them to local internships, educating them about debt, connecting them to related trade schools, and overall equipping them for the educational and financial path ahead. Seamless and centralized communication results in informed decisions, a broadened workforce, and a career-focused graduating class. We want to help students navigate this complex world of post-secondary education. At the heart of CUED-In’s mission is the connections to work-based learning opportunities and career exploration. In our discussions with students, business owners, and school administrators we’ve discovered common issues that result from a lack of centralized communication. Majority of students are graduating without experience in the workforce, let alone a foundation for a career path. Students are unaware or uninformed about local employment. Both schools and businesses look for ways to bridge the communication gap, but connecting students to businesses adds time to an already full workday. Through our app and web-based platform, CUED-In bridges this gap between education and business to fill local vacancies, promote continuing education, and build relationships. For more information please email us at info@cued-in.com
